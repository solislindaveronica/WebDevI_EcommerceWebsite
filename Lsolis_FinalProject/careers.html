Carpenter
A carpenter is a professional who works with crews to build structures made out of wood, adjust and repair wood projects in various projects. Work with their hands, using tools to build and install frameworks that last a lifetime!
Reading blueprints, drawings and sketches to fully grasp requirements
Taking measurements and calculating the size and amount of material needed
Cutting, shaping and smoothing lumber and other material (e.g. fiberglass) according to measurements and the use of technologies
We need carpenters to be creative and dedicated
position is remote
must have his/her woodshop with tools
Must be able to work with minimal supervision
Live in the US
pay arrangements can be made by hr, salary, contract, or project.
